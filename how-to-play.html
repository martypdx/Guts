How to Play

Scoring:

  • Aces are high.
  • Any pair beats any non-pair.
  • A higher pair beats a lower pair.
  • Between two non-pairs, the higher card wins.
  • When both player have the same high card, the other card determines the winner.

Playing Guts:

  1. Press the button to start a new round and deal the cards.
  2. Choose or to play your hand or fold your cards.
  3. If you're IN: get 2 points if you beat your opponent; lose 2 points if you're beaten.
  4. If you're OUT: lose 1 point.
  5. When the deck runs out, whoever has more points wins.
